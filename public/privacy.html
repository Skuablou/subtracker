Privacy Policy

SubTracker — Last updated: March 27, 2025

1. Overview

SubTracker ("we", "our", or "the app") helps you track your recurring subscriptions and monthly expenses. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the data you provide to us directly:

  • Account information – your email address, used for authentication via Supabase.
  • Subscription data – names, amounts, billing dates, and categories of subscriptions you enter.
  • Payment information – if you upgrade to Premium, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not store your card details.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, microphone, or files.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not run ads.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and save your subscription data across devices.
  • To process Premium payments via Stripe.
  • To provide and improve the SubTracker service.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Stripe – payment processing. Privacy Policy
  • Vercel – hosting. Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure database storage. However, no system is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

SubTracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or within the app.
